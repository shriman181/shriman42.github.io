Founder

Anirudh Ojha
Founder & CEO · Traverse
Anirudh brings deep operational experience at the intersection of industrial energy, sustainability compliance, and enterprise software. Before founding Traverse, he worked directly with large industrial enterprises navigating the first wave of mandatory ESG disclosures in India — and saw firsthand how compliance chaos, energy inefficiency, and reactive maintenance compounded each other with no integrated solution in sight.

He founded Traverse on a singular thesis: enterprises under mandatory sustainability mandates deserve a platform that handles carbon accounting, grid optimization, energy auditing, and asset intelligence in one unified system — not four vendors and a spreadsheet.

The Founding Thesis
Every enterprise now has a mandatory sustainability mandate. The tools to fulfill it are fragmented across four categories — carbon, energy, auditing, and maintenance — each served by a different vendor with no shared data layer. Traverse eliminates that fragmentation.
Why APAC First
India has the most energy-intensive industrial base in Asia outside China, the most complex utility landscape (DISCOM patchwork), and a regulatory framework — BRSR — that US tools simply don't understand. Being native to this environment is a structural moat, not just a go-to-market choice.
Long-Term Vision
Traverse becomes the climate operating system for the industrial enterprise globally — the infrastructure layer that connects every energy-consuming asset to the carbon accounting and reporting layer that boards, regulators, and investors demand.
